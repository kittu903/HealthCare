Continuing Education

Purchase AHEC partners with professional organizations to help bring learning opportunities to health care professionals in the region.

RESOURCES FOR HEALTHCARE PROVIDERS

Dental continuing education courses are coordinated and co-sponsored annually with Purchase Dental Society and the West Kentucky Community & Technical College Dental Assisting/Dental Hygiene Program to help meet the educational needs of area dentists and hygienists.
